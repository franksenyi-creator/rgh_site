Reflective Genesis Hypothesis(RGH)

Consciousness Time and the Structure of the Universe

What is RGH?

The universe exists as a self-reflective loop across time - reality is not created once, but is continuously generated by future consciousness reaching back to shape its own past. In simpler terms: Humans (and consciousness itself) are not just products of the universe - they are participants in its ongoing creation, retroactively influencing the conditions that allowed them to exist.
